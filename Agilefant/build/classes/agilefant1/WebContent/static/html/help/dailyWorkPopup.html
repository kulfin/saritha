My work view is a personal page for every user, which gathers all your current tasks and stories into a single view.


The Load graph calculates the assigned load for a particular user and shows the estimate on how much work the user has on average in the coming days.

With the task and story queues, you can prioritize your tasks and inform other users what you are currently or in the near future working on. For the tasks in your task queue, your name appears as bold font in the other views which show your tasks (for example, the iteration view).
